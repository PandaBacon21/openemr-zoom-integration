| TD-01 | Flask Backend |    L | Backlog | Medium   | Recurring-meeting / shared Zoom meeting support | `MeetingRecord.zoom_meeting_id` is the string PK, which forces 1 MeetingRecord per Zoom meeting and breaks if a single Zoom meeting is reused across multiple appointments/encounters (recurring telehealth, shared room links). Tactical mitigation in place today: `MeetingRecord.clinical_note` `order_by="ClinicalNoteRecord.received_at.desc()"` makes the single-appointment-multiple-notes case deterministic. |
